CHICKEN BIRYANI

SERVES: 4

PREP TIME: 30 MINS

COOK TIME: 1 HOUR

Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Green Chilies (slit)
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Coriander Leaves (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Mint Leaves (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 4 Cloves
  • 4 Green Cardamoms
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 2 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • Salt to Taste
  • 4 cups Water
  • A few saffron strands soaked in warm milk
